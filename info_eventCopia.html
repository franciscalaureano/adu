TAUC & Sofia Escobar Orchestra

4th of december

06:00pm - 07:00pm

Convento São Francisco
Avenida da Guarda Inglesa, Coimbra (1,5km)

10€

+ 3 go

Description

On her first visit to Coimbra, the renowned soloist Sofia Escobar and the singer João Cipriano Martins join the Orchestra of Tuna Académica da Universidade de Coimbra to present a concert that highlights Musical Theatre. This show, which will take place in the Grand Auditorium of the Convento são Francisco, will be performed by one of the best Musical Theatre interpreters in the United Kingdom and Spain, also showing a never before seen side of the interpretation of Coimbra songs, in a magical moment for all ages.
